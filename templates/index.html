How FindMyPerson Works: The High-Accuracy 4-Phase System

We combine crucial information provided by guardians and police with cutting-edge AI to maximize the chances of a fast reunion. Every step is designed for maximum speed and certainty.

Phase 1 & 2: Input and AI Training

1. Registration & Immediate Report

The guardian registers the case via the police department. They immediately receive an email confirmation with the unique Complaint ID and a detailed printable report for their records.

2. High-Accuracy AI Training (Guardian Input)

Guardians upload up to 5 high-resolution images. These images are used to train our ML model, creating a robust feature profile for the missing person, ensuring maximum accuracy during the live search.

Phase 3 & 4: Live Search and Resolution

3. Live CCTV & Camera Feed Matching

The trained ML profile is deployed to continuously scan CCTV and camera live feeds. Upon detecting a match, the system generates a location pin in real-time.

4. Police Alert & Status Update

The nearest police station is instantly alerted with the person's precise location. Once the case is resolved, the status is updated, and the guardian is notified and can track the progress online.

Frequently Asked Questions

These answers address common concerns from guardians, citizens, and police personnel regarding reporting, data, technology, and case resolution protocols.

  • How quickly does the search begin after I file a report?

    The search is instantaneous. Once the police verify and submit the case, our AI engine begins scanning live and archival data within minutes. You receive your unique Complaint ID immediately via email, and the local department receives an automated alert to initiate ground protocols.

  • What kind of photos provide the best matching accuracy?

    For the best results, upload 5 high-resolution images showing the missing person's face clearly. These should ideally include: 1) a close-up frontal photo, 2) a profile photo, and 3) a full-body photo. Our system uses these images to train the ML model for maximum feature recognition.

  • Can I track the progress of my case online?

    Yes. Using your Complaint ID on the "Check Status" page, you can see the case's current status (e.g., Pending Verification, Active Search, Resolved). This provides real-time transparency regarding the police investigation efforts.

  • How does FindMyPerson use CCTV and prioritize privacy?

    The system only uses anonymized feature templates for searching and does not store identifiable civilian footage. Data is secured using advanced encryption techniques, and access is strictly limited to verified police officers (Department Login) for official duties only.

  • As a police officer, how does this system assist my duties?

    The system provides three major benefits: 1) Instant Reporting (eliminating paperwork), 2) Real-Time Alerts (locating subjects using live camera feeds), and 3) Unified Data (a central dashboard to view all cases and updates). It provides technology to significantly speed up your investigation time.

  • What should I do if I find a match in the public area?

    If you believe you have located a missing person listed on our public database, DO NOT approach the individual. Immediately contact your local police station and reference the Complaint ID displayed with the person's profile.
